Gayatri Mantra (Rig Veda)

Om Bhur Bhuvah Swaha, Tat Savitur Varenyam, Bhargo Devasya Dhīmahi, Dhiyo Yo Nah Prachodayāt.

The supreme mantra of wisdom and enlightenment.

Maha Mrityunjaya Mantra (Rig Veda)

Om Tryambakam Yajamahe Sugandhim Pushtivardhanam, Urvarukamiva Bandhanan Mrityor Mukshiya Maamritat.

A powerful healing mantra for longevity and protection.

Purusha Suktam (Rig Veda)

Sahasraśīrṣā puruṣaḥ, Sahasrākṣaḥ sahasrapāt...

The cosmic hymn describing the Supreme Being and creation.

Shri Suktam (Yajur Veda)

Hiranyavarnaṁ harinīṁ suvarnarajatasrajām...

Mantra for wealth and blessings of Goddess Lakshmi.

Durga Suktam (Sama Veda)

Jatavedase sunavama somamaratīyato nidahati vedah...

For protection and strength from Goddess Durga.

Narayana Suktam (Atharva Veda)

Sahasraśīrṣaṁ devaṁ viśvākśaṁ viśvaśambhuvam...

For devotion and realization of the Supreme Lord Narayana.

Rudram Namakam & Chamakam (Yajur Veda)

Om Namo Bhagavate Rudraya...

Powerful chant to invoke the blessings of Lord Shiva.

Medha Suktam (Rig Veda)

Medha devī juṣamāṇā na āgaad viśvāci bhadrā...

For wisdom, intelligence, and divine knowledge.

Shanti Mantra (Atharva Veda)

Om Sahana Vavatu, Sahanau Bhunaktu...

For universal peace and harmony.

Agni Suktam (Rig Veda)

Om Agnim-Ile Purohitam...

Dedicated to Agni, the divine fire energy.

Vishnu Sahasranama

Om Vishvam Vishnur Vashatkaro...

1,000 names of Lord Vishnu for divine blessings.

Glorifies Goddess Durga as the divine mother.

Powerful hymn for strength, devotion, and protection.
